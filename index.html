EcoCanopy

A Website About Enviromental Conservation

EcoCanopy is a foundation founded in the heart of Kenya in the year 2005.


Our aim was to protect the endangered Maumau forest but we soon became a worldwide organisation saving forests and endangered species all over the world


We now have branches in every country of the world





Our Greatest Achivements

  • We removed a hundred tonnes of waste from the Pacific Ocean,the Antlantic Ocean and the Indian Ocean

  • We used our donations to build 10 schools in a developing country in Asia

  • We restored the habitat of the endangered Yellow Eyed Owl which was on the brink of extinction

  • We saved the MauMau Forest in Kenya which was 80% cut down

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a greener environment for generations to come

Why We Started

  • To Communicate To Communities Around The Globe About Environmental Awareness

  • To Promote Environmental Awareness Among Communities All Over The World

  • To Do Our Part In Reversing Climate Change

  • To Protect The Habitat Of Animals Which We Love So Much
